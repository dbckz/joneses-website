Our Story

Born from a love of The Smiths

In October 2016, five musicians came together with a shared obsession: recreating the magic of The Smiths. Not as parody, not as pastiche, but as a genuine tribute to one of Britain's most influential bands.

Every performance is a labour of love—from the jangle of Johnny Marr's guitar to the theatrical presence of Morrissey at his peak. We don't just play the songs. We inhabit them.
